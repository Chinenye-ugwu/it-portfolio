# Week 1 – Personal and Professional Foundation

## Overview

Week 1 marked the beginning of my IT497 Capstone journey. During this week, I reflected on my educational journey, career transition, and personal values while establishing the foundation for both my Spiritual Progression Portfolio and my Professional Growth Portfolio.

Coming from more than ten years in the insurance industry, I documented my reasons for pursuing Information Technology and identified the experiences and skills that would support my transition into System Administration.

---

## Spiritual Progression Portfolio

This week focused on identifying the spiritual principles that have guided my educational and professional journey.

Some of the key topics included:

- Trusting God throughout career transitions.
- Seeking personal and spiritual growth.
- Developing faith while learning new technical skills.
- Establishing a habit of prayer and personal scripture study.

These reflections became the foundation for the guiding principles that I continued developing in later weeks.

---

## Professional Growth Portfolio

I began planning my professional portfolio by documenting:

- My career transition from insurance to Information Technology.
- My educational goals at Ensign College.
- My long-term interest in System Administration and Cloud Support.
- My existing leadership, customer service, and documentation experience.

This week also provided the opportunity to begin refining my résumé and identifying technical projects that would later become part of my professional portfolio.

---

## Key Learning Outcomes

During Week 1, I learned that professional growth involves much more than developing technical knowledge. Building a successful career also requires integrity, humility, continuous learning, and a willingness to adapt to new challenges.

I also recognized that my previous professional experience continues to provide valuable transferable skills that strengthen my development as an Information Technology student.

---

## Reflection

Completing the first week of the capstone helped me recognize that my spiritual life and professional development are closely connected. My faith provides purpose and direction while my studies continue to strengthen my confidence and technical abilities.

Looking ahead, I am committed to continuous improvement and lifelong learning as I prepare for a career in Information Technology.

## Introduction

Week 1 marked the beginning of my IT 497 Capstone journey. It gave me the opportunity to reflect on my academic progress, career transition, spiritual development, and professional goals as I prepare for a career in Information Technology.

During this week, I began building both my Spiritual Growth Portfolio and Professional Growth Portfolio. These portfolios will continue to grow throughout the capstone course and serve as evidence of my technical development, professional readiness, and personal values.

---

## SMART Goals

One of the first activities in Week 1 was creating SMART goals that will guide my development throughout IT 497.

### Goal 1 – Strengthen My Technical Skills

Continue developing practical skills in Linux administration, Windows Server, Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), networking, cybersecurity, and systems administration through coursework, hands-on projects, and independent learning.

### Goal 2 – Build a Professional Portfolio

Develop a professional portfolio that documents my academic projects, certifications, technical reports, résumé, and reflective learning throughout my System Administration program.

### Goal 3 – Successfully Transition into Information Technology

Prepare for an entry-level career in System Administration or Cloud Support by combining my technical education with more than ten years of leadership and customer service experience gained in the insurance industry.

These goals provide a clear roadmap for my continued academic, professional, and personal development throughout the capstone course.

## Introduction

Week 2 focused on strengthening both my Spiritual Progression Portfolio and Professional Growth Portfolio. During this stage of my capstone journey, I reflected more deeply on the principles that guide my life, evaluated my career goals, and began planning how my portfolio would demonstrate my growth throughout the Information Technology program.

This week also encouraged me to think beyond technical skills by considering professional ethics, lifelong learning, and how my personal values influence the way I approach technology and service.

## Spiritual Growth

During Week 2, I spent time strengthening the spiritual foundation that supports both my personal life and professional development. I reflected on how faith influences the decisions I make, the way I approach challenges, and the person I am becoming throughout my education.

One scripture that had a significant impact on me this week was **Proverbs 3:5–6**, which teaches us to trust in the Lord rather than relying solely on our own understanding. This scripture reminded me that although my transition into Information Technology has presented many new challenges, I do not have to face them alone. Trusting God has given me confidence to continue learning, even when I encounter unfamiliar technologies or difficult assignments.

As I continue developing my technical knowledge, I also want to strengthen qualities such as humility, integrity, patience, and perseverance. These attributes not only help me grow spiritually but also prepare me to become a dependable and ethical Information Technology professional.

### New Spiritual Resources

During Week 2, I added several resources to my Spiritual Progression Portfolio, including:

- Proverbs 3:5–6
- Elder David A. Bednar's teachings on bringing all aspects of life together through Christ
- Personal prayer and scripture study
- Personal reflection on my career transition
- Experiences that strengthened my faith while learning new technical skills

These resources continue to remind me that spiritual growth and professional development are not separate journeys but complementary parts of becoming the person I hope to be.

## Professional Development

Week 2 provided an opportunity to further develop my Professional Growth Portfolio by refining my career goals, strengthening my résumé, and identifying projects that demonstrate both technical competence and personal growth.

As I reflected on my professional journey, I recognized how the leadership, communication, customer service, and organizational skills I developed during more than ten years in the insurance industry continue to support my transition into Information Technology. These experiences have prepared me to work effectively with people while learning the technical skills required for a career in System Administration.

During this week, I also began organizing evidence that will become part of my professional portfolio, including my résumé, technical projects, certifications, and career goals. Rather than simply collecting assignments, I am building a portfolio that demonstrates continuous learning and professional growth.

### Professional Portfolio Progress

During Week 2, I continued developing:

- My professional résumé
- Technical skills inventory
- Career goals
- Portfolio planning
- Project documentation
- Professional reflection
- Evidence of technical growth

Each addition strengthened the foundation of a portfolio that I can continue expanding throughout my academic program and eventually share with potential employers.

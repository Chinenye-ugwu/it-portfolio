# Career Goals

## My Vision

My goal is to build a successful career in Information Technology by combining technical knowledge with integrity, continuous learning, and a commitment to serving others.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology – System Administration at Ensign College. Through my coursework and hands-on projects, I continue developing practical skills in Linux administration, Windows Server, Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), networking, cybersecurity, and cloud technologies.

## Short-Term Goals

- Begin my IT career as a System Administrator or Cloud Support professional.
- Continue strengthening my Linux and Windows Server administration skills.
- Gain practical experience with Microsoft Azure and AWS.
- Earn additional industry-recognized certifications.
- Build a strong portfolio demonstrating technical projects and continuous learning.

## Long-Term Goals

My long-term objective is to become a highly skilled Cloud Systems Administrator or Cloud Engineer who designs, secures, and manages reliable technology environments.

I also hope to mentor others entering Information Technology, just as experienced professionals have encouraged me throughout my own transition into the field.

I believe that lifelong learning, integrity, and service will remain the foundation of my professional journey.

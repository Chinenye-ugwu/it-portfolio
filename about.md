# About Me

## Welcome

Hello! My name is **Chinenye Edith Ugwu**, and I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in **Information Technology – System Administration** at **Ensign College**.

After spending more than ten years building a successful career in the insurance industry, I made the decision to transition into Information Technology. While changing careers has been challenging, it has also been one of the most rewarding decisions I have made. Every new concept I learn strengthens my confidence and brings me one step closer to achieving my professional goals.

## My Journey

My background in insurance helped me develop valuable skills in leadership, customer service, communication, documentation, problem-solving, and working with people from diverse backgrounds. As I study Information Technology, I continue to build technical skills in Linux administration, Windows Server, Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), networking, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.

Although I entered the technology field later than many others, I believe that determination, continuous learning, and perseverance are among my greatest strengths.

## My Values

I believe that technology should be used to serve people responsibly. My Christian faith reminds me to approach every opportunity with integrity, humility, compassion, and a willingness to keep learning.

These values influence not only how I study but also the kind of IT professional I hope to become.

## My Goal

My career goal is to begin as a **System Administrator** or **Cloud Support** professional, where I can help organizations build secure, reliable, and efficient technology environments. Over time, I hope to continue developing my expertise in cloud technologies, automation, and cybersecurity while remaining committed to lifelong learning and professional excellence.
